Campaign Highlights

About Basilio

Basilio Kirimi is a young, vibrant, and articulate leader vying for the seat of South Imenti Member of Parliament in Meru County. He previously demonstrated his courage and commitment to the people by contesting for the Senate seat of Meru County.

Vision & Beliefs

Basilio believes the current MP has lost touch with the common mwananchi and has done little for South Imenti. He is committed to being a voice for the people and ensuring that government policies are aligned with the needs of the citizens. He strongly supports Hon. Dr. Fred Matiang'i as a potential president in 2027.

Campaign Slogan

"One Term" – for the incumbent MP and the government of Kenya.

Why Vote for Basilio?

  • Well-articulated and knowledgeable on national and local politics.
  • Deep understanding of the plights and aspirations of South Imenti residents.
  • Committed to accountability, transparency, and progress for the people.
  • Represents youth, energy, and a new vision for leadership.
