Campaign Highlights

About Basilio Kirimi

Basilio Kirimi is a bold, articulate, and people-centered leader vying for the South Imenti Member of Parliament seat under the Kenya Social Congress party.

He previously contested for the Meru County Senate seat, demonstrating courage, integrity, and commitment to public service.

Leadership Philosophy

Basilio believes leadership must serve the people, not personal interests. Accountability, transparency, and development are his guiding principles.

Vision for South Imenti

  • Transparent use of NG-CDF funds
  • Youth and women economic empowerment
  • Improved education and bursaries
  • Support for farmers and small businesses

Campaign Slogan

“One Term” — accountability for failed leadership.
