Campaign Highlights

About Basilio Kirimi

Basilio Kirimi is a bold, articulate, and people-centered leader vying for the South Imenti Member of Parliament seat under the Kenya Social Congress party. He is widely respected for his courage, integrity, and consistency in speaking for the common mwananchi.

Basilio previously contested for the Meru County Senate seat, demonstrating rare political bravery and commitment to public service. His leadership journey is founded on accountability, transparency, and meaningful development.

Leadership Philosophy

Basilio believes leadership must serve the people, not personal interests. He advocates for responsible use of public funds, youth empowerment, and restoring dignity to public office.

Vision for South Imenti

  • Transparent and accountable use of NG-CDF funds
  • Empowering youth and women economically
  • Improving access to education and bursaries
  • Supporting farmers and small businesses
  • Improving infrastructure and public services

National Outlook

Basilio strongly believes Hon. Dr. Fred Matiang’i has the discipline and vision required to lead Kenya in 2027. He supports leadership grounded in integrity, service delivery, and respect for institutions.

Campaign Slogan

“One Term” — accountability for failed leadership.
